        Healthcare ERP Benchmarks

        • Implementation duration: Many healthcare ERP transformations require 18–30 months depending on scope, modules, integrations, and operating model complexity.
        • SME allocation: Functional SMEs often need 40–60% allocation during design, testing, training, and go-live readiness.
        • Hypercare: Organizations should plan for 60–90 days of focused stabilization support after go-live.
        • Integrations: Large healthcare ERP programs may involve dozens to 100+ integrations across finance, HR, supply chain, identity, revenue cycle, and clinical systems.
        • Change management: Effective adoption planning often begins 6–9 months before go-live, not during training.
